Research & Innovation

Field Research & Market Validation

Our team conducted extensive on-ground market validation by visiting dairy farms across Coimbatore, Erode, and Tiruppur districts. We analyzed farmer workflows, common challenges in cattle health, and adoption rates of existing technologies. Insights from these field visits helped shape our product design, features, and problem focus. The direct interaction with over 50+ farmers gave us strong validation for early mastitis detection solutions.

IoT-Based Monitoring Study

We explored IoT technologies in dairy health by studying sensor hardware, data collection mechanisms, and real-time analytics. Our research focused on identifying the most efficient and affordable sensors suited for rural farms. We also analyzed their power usage, accuracy in detecting temperature/motion, and wireless transmission reliability. This helped us design an optimized prototype for on-farm mastitis monitoring with minimal maintenance.

Startup Showcasing & Collaboration

We presented our AgriTech solution at platforms like StartupTN and TANUVAS-VIF, receiving constructive feedback and support. These opportunities helped refine our business model, validate our innovation from veterinary experts, and gain exposure to real stakeholders. Pitching at such forums also connected us with domain mentors and early adopters interested in piloting our tech. These collaborations boosted our confidence and set the stage for future scaling and research grants.
