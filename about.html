Who We Are

Thalir Tech is a forward-thinking, student-led AgriTech startup focused on harnessing the power of technology to drive innovation in the dairy sector. We are passionate about supporting farmers—especially small-scale farmers—with intelligent tools that improve livestock health, enhance productivity, and promote sustainable farming practices.

By combining data science and AI, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional dairy farming and modern digital solutions.

Our Mission

To empower dairy farmers with reliable, easy-to-use, and affordable health monitoring solutions that enable early disease detection, reduce economic losses, and ensure better animal welfare — all while respecting data privacy and on-farm independence.

Our Vision

Healthier herds. Smarter farms. Stronger communities.
